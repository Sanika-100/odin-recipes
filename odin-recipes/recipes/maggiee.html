Recipe of Maggiee

Description

Maggi is the classic Indian comfort food loved by children and adults alike. It is a quick and easy instant noodle dish prepared within minutes, making it a favorite for busy days or late-night cravings.

This recipe shows you how to cook Maggi perfectly with vegetables and spices for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Maggi noodles
  • 1 and 1/2 cups water
  • 1 Maggi tastemaker spice mix
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1 tsp oil or butter

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions, tomatoes, and capsicum for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add water and bring it to a boil.
  3. Break the Maggi noodles and add them to the boiling water.
  4. Sprinkle the tastemaker masala over the noodles.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the water is absorbed and noodles are soft.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
